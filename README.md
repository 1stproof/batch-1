# First Proof — First Batch

A set of ten research-level math questions to evaluate the capabilities of AI systems to autonomously solve problems that arise naturally in the research process.

Questions are drawn from algebraic combinatorics, spectral graph theory, algebraic topology, stochastic analysis, symplectic geometry, representation theory, lattices in Lie groups, tensor analysis, and numerical linear algebra.

## Contents

- `First_Proof.tex` — LaTeX source of the paper, including problem statements
- `FirstProofSolutionsComments.pdf` — Solutions and commentary (released February 13, 2026)
- `Supplementary Files/` — Encrypted archive of original solutions and AI logs
